The problem

You're spending real money on marketing and still can't answer one question

What is $1 of your marketing actually worth on the other side? Most owners can't answer it cleanly — and it's not their fault. Facebook claims one number, Google claims another, your CRM shows a third, and all three are technically "right." The gap between what you spend and what you can prove came back is exactly where the money disappears. In regulated, high-LTV businesses it's worse: the platforms can't legally hand you clean attribution, the tracking has to stay compliant, and most agencies hide it behind a vanity-metric dashboard. So you keep funding a number you can't see.

  • You approve ad spend on faith, not on a traced impression-to-revenue number
  • Three platforms report three different truths, and none of them tie to deposits
  • Your team is buried in manual intake, follow-up, and reporting that AI could absorb
  • Your agency sends you impressions and clicks — never payback period or ROAS (return on ad spend) to the P&L
